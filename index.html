About

My name is Vanessa, I'm the owner of Nisma, which was founded in 2023.

I've been tattooing since June 2020, starting on my family and friends. I slowly started tattooing people I knew less and less.
Now I find it crazy that people that don't even know me get tattoos from me. It feels surreal sometimes.

I have many dreams and goals in my life, and one of them is being able to tattoo whenever I want, without having to worry about anything else.

Hope you enjoy my content!
